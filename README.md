🎮 SoloFlap – Flappy Bird × Solo Leveling
By Abdul Kabeer

Step into a system‑awakened world where a lone bird must defy gravity.
SoloFlap is a fast‑paced arcade reimagining of the classic Flappy Bird, wrapped in the dark, glowing aesthetics of Solo Leveling.

🕹️ Tap, click, or press space to keep the bird airborne and weave through endless green pipes.
⚡ Earn points with every gate you pass, and watch the screen flash with power‑like particle effects.
📊 A built‑in scoreboard saves your top 10 runs locally — challenge yourself to break your own record.
💻 A sleek “System” loading screen greets you on launch, complete with a progress bar and boot‑sequence logs, as if you’re entering a hunter’s interface.
❌ Close button included — exit the game anytime, no clutter.

Simple one‑tap gameplay meets a stylish, game‑like UI. Perfect for quick sessions or sharing with friends.
